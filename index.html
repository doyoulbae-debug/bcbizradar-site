Questions buyers ask

Where does the data come from?

BC Corporate Registry weekly notices (published under the King's Printer Licence) and the City of Vancouver business-licence open dataset (Open Government Licence – Vancouver). Both official, public, and licensed for commercial reuse with attribution — printed on every issue.

Is cold outreach to these businesses legal?

CASL permits commercial email to addresses conspicuously published without a no-solicitation notice, where your message is relevant to the recipient's role (s.10(9)(b) implied consent) — always with identification and unsubscribe. We ship the published-source URL for every contact so your basis is documented.

Why are some rows missing contact info?

Because the company is days old and hasn't published any yet. That's the point — reach them at formation, or wait for our re-scan to append contacts as they appear. Vendors showing 100% email coverage on day-0 incorporations are selling guesses.

Can I get just my city or niche? How do I cancel?

Yes — that's the custom list build, and subscription slices can be scoped on request. Cancelling is one click, any time, effective end of the billing period. No contracts.
